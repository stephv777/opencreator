# Publish

Publishing should never require recreating work.
OpenCreator prepares the appropriate formats automatically.
The creator simply chooses where to publish.

---

Preferred destinations
Open networks
Decentralized platforms
Community services

---

Optional destinations
Commercial platforms
Social networks
Video platforms
Cloud services

---

OpenCreator prepares:

✔ thumbnails
✔ subtitles
✔ descriptions
✔ keywords
✔ metadata

whenever possible.

The creator always approves before publication.

---

Publishing is optional.

Preservation is not.
