## Why OpenCreator exists

Digital creation should be simple.

Today, creators spend too much time installing software, converting files, managing backups, publishing on multiple platforms and adapting to constantly changing online services.

OpenCreator exists to reverse that relationship.

Technology should adapt to creators.

Creators should never have to adapt to technology.

OpenCreator is not another application.

It is a local-first environment built on free software and open standards.

Its purpose is to connect existing open-source tools into one coherent, calm and human-centered experience.

Everything begins on your own computer.

Your creations remain yours to preserve.

From there, OpenCreator helps you convert, organize and publish your work wherever you choose.

OpenCreator always gives priority to:

- local storage
- free software
- open standards
- decentralized networks
- community protocols

Commercial platforms remain available as optional destinations, never as the center of the project.

## Our mission
