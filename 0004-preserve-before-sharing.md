# Welcome

Welcome to OpenCreator.

OpenCreator is not another application.
It is an environment designed to simplify digital creation.
Instead of learning dozens of software applications, file formats and online platforms, creators simply express what they want to achieve.
OpenCreator prepares the appropriate tools, preserves projects locally and facilitates publication whenever requested.
Technology should become almost invisible.
The creator remains at the center.
---

## Our mission

Create.
Preserve.
Transmit.
Everything else is only a tool.

---

## Who is OpenCreator for?

OpenCreator is designed for:

- writers
- teachers
- artists
- photographers
- filmmakers
- musicians
- podcasters
- developers
- associations
- independent creators

No technical knowledge is required.

---

## OpenCreator principles

- Local first.
- Open first.
- Simplicity.
- Preservation before sharing.
- Community.
- Freedom of choice.
