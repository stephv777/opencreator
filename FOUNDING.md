# Founding

OpenCreator began with a simple observation.
Modern creators spend too much time managing technology.
Creating should remain the priority.
Technology should quietly support the creative process.
OpenCreator was imagined to connect the best Free/Open Source tools into one coherent environment.
Instead of asking creators to adapt to software,
OpenCreator adapts software to creators.
