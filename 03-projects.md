# Projects

Everything begins with a Project.

A Project is a complete container.

It keeps together:

- documents
- images
- videos
- audio
- books
- subtitles
- thumbnails
- versions
- metadata
- publication history
- backups

Nothing should exist outside a Project.

---

Projects remain readable.
Projects remain portable.
Projects remain durable.

---

OpenCreator remembers every important step.
Previous versions may always be restored.
The creator never loses work.

---

The creator creates.
OpenCreator preserves.
OpenCreator converts.
OpenCreator publishes.

OpenCreator remembers.
