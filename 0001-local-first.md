# Decision 0001 — Local First

Status: Accepted

## Context

Creators should never depend on an online service to access their own work.

Cloud services are useful, but they should extend local creation rather than replace it.

## Decision

Every OpenCreator project is stored locally first.

Publishing, synchronization and online collaboration are optional services built on top of local storage.

## Consequences

- Projects remain accessible without Internet.
- Users always keep a complete copy of their creations.
- Backups become simpler.
- Migration to another platform remains possible.
