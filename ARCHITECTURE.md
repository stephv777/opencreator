# OpenCreator Architecture

OpenCreator is not a single application.
It is an environment composed of independent layers.
Each layer has one responsibility.
---

## Layer 1 — Foundation

Operating system
(Debian Stable)
Provides stability, hardware support and long-term reliability.
---

## Layer 2 — Core Services

Storage
Networking
Containers
Synchronization
Identity
Backups
These services remain mostly invisible to creators.
---

## Layer 3 — OpenCreator Environment

The OpenCreator dashboard.
Project management.
Workflow orchestration.
Software selection.
Automation.

This is the heart of OpenCreator.
---

## Layer 4 — Creative Applications

Writing
Images
Video
Audio
Books
Websites
Development

These applications remain replaceable.
OpenCreator does not depend on one specific software.
---

## Layer 5 — Publication

Local storage
External drives
IPFS
P2P
PeerTube
Websites
Commercial platforms
Publication always remains optional.

---

## Layer 6 — Community

Documentation
Translations
Contributions

Shared knowledge

OpenCreator grows through its community.
