# Decision 0002 — Open First

Status: Accepted

## Context

OpenCreator aims to preserve digital creations for many years.

Open standards provide greater durability than proprietary ecosystems.

## Decision

Whenever possible OpenCreator gives priority to:

- Open standards
- Free/Open Source Software
- Open formats
- Decentralized protocols
- Community driven services

Commercial platforms remain optional destinations.

They are never the foundation of OpenCreator.

## Consequences

OpenCreator remains independent from commercial strategies while still allowing creators to publish everywhere.
